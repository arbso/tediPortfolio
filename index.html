About me

I’m Teidi, a young Video Editor based on Albania. I have and still do work across many aspects of video&film, right through from pre-production to post production.

I am 21 years old and I study at the Academy of Film and Multimedia Marubi for the directing and editing profile.

For 6 years I have not stopped working on various film projects as a production assistant in the project "Who Is De Mole" (Netherlands) and others. In addition to these, I have participated in various festivals such as "48 Hour Film Project" where I won the award "The Best Editing".

During this period of 6 years, I have made various music videos as well as TV Commercials mainly focused on editing, but I have also performed other roles such as assistant, sound engineer, camera operator, screenwriter, director and other positions.

Throughout my career I’ve spent a lot of time working within both cinema and television standards, and creating social media content or music videos with the forever changing and demanding styles of editing.

POST PRODUCTION

Although I’d like to be shooting 8 days a week, it’s not always possible to book that many cinematography jobs. When I’m not on set I like to book myself post-production work to both keep me busy, and also to keep me sharp with the fast-changing post-production landscape. I’m very familiar with Adobe Premiere Pro, FCPX, Avid Media Composer, Adobe After Effects, and DaVinci Resolve.
